Simple Summary: We don't collect any personal data. Your location stays on your device and is never sent anywhere.

Information We Collect

Salah - Prayer & Qibla does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

We believe in complete privacy. Unlike other apps, we don't use analytics, advertising networks, or data collection services.

Location Data

The app uses your device's location exclusively to:

  • Calculate accurate prayer times for your geographic area
  • Determine the precise Qibla direction toward Mecca
  • Cache your location for faster app startup

Important: Your location data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers, third parties, or anyone else.

Data Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Your last known coordinates (for offline functionality)
  • Calculated prayer times (to avoid recalculation)
  • App preferences (notification settings, etc.)

This data never leaves your device and is automatically deleted if you uninstall the app.

Permissions

Location Services: Required to calculate prayer times and Qibla direction. Used only when the app is open.

Notifications: Optional. Used only to alert you of prayer times if you enable this feature.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use:

  • Analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media SDKs
  • Crash reporting services
  • Any other third-party data collection tools

Children's Privacy

Our app is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Data Security

Since we don't collect or transmit any data, there are no data security concerns related to our servers. All processing happens locally on your device using Apple's secure iOS frameworks.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there's no data to access, modify, or delete on our end. You can:

  • Clear app data by reinstalling the app
  • Disable location access in iOS Settings
  • Turn off notifications at any time

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices (which is unlikely given our privacy-first approach), we will update this policy and notify users through app updates.
